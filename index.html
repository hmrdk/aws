Introduction to Amazon Web Services (AWS)

Join this first workshop and be part of an intimate crowd of only 5 seats!

Amazon Web Services is by far the largest and most widely used Cloud provider. It is however also a pretty complex suite of services and tools, and the pricing is hard to interpret for most people.

This half-day workshop takes you through the different services with focus on the ones well-suited for Web development. Integration examples are shown in PHP and each participant's current setup is discussed.
